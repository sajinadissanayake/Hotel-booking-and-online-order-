Golden Dragon Restaurant

An unforgettable, gastronomic adventure around the globe.


Dining is a continuous pleasure at the five star Moonlight Hotel Colombo with an array of different culinary options to choose from. with the most expansive international buffet to be found in Sri Lanka with modern and sophisticated setting, offering a peerless dining experience. Features à la minute cuisine, freshly cooked right in front of guests in purpose built action stations.
Golden Dragon takes you on an unassumingly worldwide journey, giving you hundreds flavors to taste. Special feast on Japanese, Chinese, Thai, Indian, Mexican and Italian and more best cuisines around the world. Also featuring mouth-watering Sri Lankan traditional dishes With a Western and Eastern twist.

Breakfast - 7.00 a.m. to 11.00 a.m.

Bloom Bee Cafe

Explore the varied tastes of gourmet food, coffee & tea


Here’s a place that celebrates the island’s love for tea and all other highs in life. Welcome to the Bloom Bee Cafe serving a range of delectable crispy pastries, a range of flavourful sandwiches and a variety of delicious cakes and desserts. Serving with melt-in-your-mouth different unique flavours for dipping.
Bloom bee also offers exclusive coffees and freshly pressed healthy juices and smoothies, while not forgetting the perfect cup of Sri Lankan tea with Classical music played by the pianist during the day and in the evening.

Opening hours

7.00 a.m. To 8.30 p.m.





Ocean Breeze Bar

Enjoy wondrous food & cocktails as you lounge by the pool


Located along the glistening pool waters, Ocean breeze bar is the ideal place to unwind and relax with your friends with rhyme of the beach vibes. The beverage list is extensive and offer variety of snacks, bites and mains on offer.

The perfect location to end the day with catching the last rays of the sun as it sinks into the horizon with fine wines from our exclusive cellars or tropical cocktails inspired by local flavors, while nibbling on great snacks and dance the night away with your favourite bands and DJ's!
