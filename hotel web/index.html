ROOMS AT HOTEL MOONLIGHT

Looking for the perfect relaxation by the picturesque western Coastline in Sri Lanka? Look no further than the amazing selection of rooms available at the moonlight hotel at panadura for you! You’ll find something for every need and taste at our wonderful 5 star seaside resort. If you’re planning to enjoy a couple’s getaway, consider one of our Deluxe Rooms or Premier Room. Families can enjoy the full comforts of luxury living by choosing one of our luxurious apartments. And if you really want to treat yourself like a royal, you can enjoy an exclusive feature-rich experience at out Presidential and Royal Suites!

DELUXE ROOM

Deluxe rooms are beautifully appointed with a contemporary design and local srilankan touches. All rooms look out over the indian ocean.The deluxe rooms at our property offer an opportunity to indulge in luxurious comforts in the most affordable ways. The deluxe room comes replete with a host of luxurious amenities along with many other exclusive facilities all in a compact yet elegant package. The abodes boast a tasteful interior design and they let you wake up to spectacular ocean views. All in all, these accommodation options arguably offers you the best value for your money amidst other accommodation options at beachfront hotels in Unawatuna.

DIAMOND SUITE

The 70 sqm Diamond Suite has a luxurious layout featuring a walk-in wardrobe with a private dressing area and an elegant marble bathroom with a sea view bathtub. The Grand royal Suite is positioned closer to the coastline resulting in magnificent uninterrupted views of the Andaman Sea.
