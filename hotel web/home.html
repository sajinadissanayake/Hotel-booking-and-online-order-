The MoonLight offers the elite standard for guests desiring the ultimate in luxurious, retreat accommodation. Offering 5 acres of thoughtfully designed grounds and immaculately maintained facilities, the beach side provides total peace and tranquility in a delightfully secluded setting. Situated only one & half hour from Katunayaka Airport. MoonLight is easily accessible for both international and local guest. The estate is perfect for groups or families seeking luxury resort with total privacy. We pride ourselves on the level of service provided, facilitating and accommodation experience like no other. Moonlight has also been well frequented by those looking for a unique venue to hold special events or corporate retreats. With an abundance of onsite opportunities for activities and events, there is scope to tailor the day exactly to your requirements. The functions held here have included birthdays, charity events, weddings and Indian wedding ceremonies, corporate launch days, conferences, and Christmas parties.
